# Waterloo Region Youth Opportunity Sources

Last verified: June 6, 2026

This inventory covers enrichment, competitions, clubs, camps, entrepreneurship,
leadership, employment, grants, scholarships and volunteering for middle- and
high-school students in Waterloo Region. It prioritizes official organization pages.
Individual openings change frequently, so source pages should be checked before
importing records into the app.

This is an exhaustive source inventory, not a claim that every short-lived event is
listed individually. Provider calendars and registration portals should be crawled or
reviewed on the refresh schedule below to discover individual opportunities.

## Import Guidance

For automated collection, use one record per specific posting rather than one record per
organization. Store the organization page as the source URL and the application page as
the external link where they differ.

Suggested refresh frequency:

- Live aggregators and municipal job boards: daily.
- Volunteer portals and library openings: weekly.
- Recurring camps, contests, scholarships and summer employment: monthly, increasing to
  weekly during their normal application season.
- School-coordinated programs: review each school term and label them clearly as requiring
  guidance-office or teacher involvement.

Before publishing an imported record, verify:

1. The application is currently open.
2. Waterloo Region residents or students are eligible.
3. The minimum age or grade is compatible with the app audience.
4. The deadline and program dates have not passed.
5. Fees, required certifications, police checks and time commitments are disclosed.
6. The URL is an official organization or application page.
